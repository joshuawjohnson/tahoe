How this plan thinks

Two fitness tiers and a dog that can't sit in a hot car. So we front-load dog-inclusive group activities on the iffy-weather days, split the group Saturday morning, and save the no-dog marquee item — the gondola — for the clear afternoon when the dog can rest in the A/C rental.

Field notes

Dog

  • Water + collapsible bowl on every hike — granite gets hot and exposed by midday.
  • Leashes required at every beach and trail here.
  • Never in a parked car — the gondola window is why the dog rests in the A/C rental.
  • Kiva and the bike path are the two best play sessions — both early-day.

Parking

  • West-shore lots — Kiva, Eagle Falls, Fallen Leaf — fill by 9–10am.
  • Be parked before 9 on weekend mornings. Not a suggestion.
  • Van Sickle (Stateline) is the exception — walkable, less of a crunch.
Eagle FallsFallen Leaf Lake

If plans slip

  • Thu/Fri rain: swap the beach/bike block for Heavenly Village or MacDuff's covered patio.
  • Parents fading at altitude: drop Sunday to just the lower Van Sickle loop, or a lakeside coffee.
  • 30s want more: parasail or a jet-ski hour Saturday before the gondola.
